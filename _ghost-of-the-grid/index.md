## About This Modlist

This modlist pushes the cyberpunk aesthetic to the maximum while improving gameplay mechanics and adding new content to explore in Night City.

### Key Features

- **Enhanced Visuals**: Improved lighting, weather effects, and cyberpunk atmosphere
- **Gameplay Improvements**: Better combat mechanics and AI behavior
- **New Content**: Additional quests, items, and customization options
- **Performance Optimizations**: Balanced for smooth gameplay
