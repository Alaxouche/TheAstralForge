# Known Issues

While you play the list, you might run into a few issues that can vary from a simple fun bug to CTDs. Let's remember that we heavily mod an unstable and extremely bugged game. From such a base, adding mods can lead to instability. Here is a compilation of most of the known issues you can find when playing.

## Vanilla Game Issues

</div>
<div lang="fr" markdown="1">

# Problèmes Connus
